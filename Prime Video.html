Yes I am Student

Yes I Am Student is a 2021 Indian Punjabi language film directed and produced by Tarnvir Singh Jagpal. It stars Sidhu Moose Wala, Mandy Takhar and Gill Raunta. The film is written by Gill Raunta. The film was released on 22 October 2021. This film marked Moose Wala's final film in his lifetime before his assassination on 29 May 2022. The soundtrack of the film are composed by Sidhu Moose Wala, The Kidd, Intense, Gurtaj and Barbie Mann. They consisted of six songs. Tracklist

Yaar Jigree Kasooti Degree

Yaar Jigree Kasooti Degree is an Indian Punjabi-language comedy-drama web-series created by Rabby Tiwana. Co-produced by Punjabi and Media International. The series marks as one of first Punjabi successful web-series on college students. The first episode of the series was aired on 15 Sep 2018, with first season concluding on 31 December 2018 at have been viewed over thirty and fifteen million times respectively.

Extraction

Extraction is a 2020 American action thriller film directed by Sam Hargrave (in his feature directorial debut) and written by Joe Russo, based on the graphic novel Ciudad by Ande Parks, Joe Russo, Anthony Russo, Fernando León González, and Eric Skillman. In the film, an Australian black ops mercenary takes on a mission to save an Indian drug lord's kidnapped son in Dhaka, Bangladesh, but the mission goes awry when he is double-crossed.

Superman

Superman is a superhero who appears in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by writer Jerry Siegel and artist Joe Shuster, and debuted in the comic book Action Comics #1. Superman was the first superhero with superpowers and arguably the first major superhero ever created. Superman has been adapted to a number of other media, which includes radio serials, novels, films, television shows, theater, and video games. Superman was born on the fictional planet Krypton and was named Kal-El.
